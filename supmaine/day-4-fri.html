The wedding begins. A slow breakfast with the crew in Cape Elizabeth, a free morning with nothing owed to anyone, then a walk-on ferry across Casco Bay to an island lobster bake at sunset. This is the day the road trip becomes the celebration.

9:00a
Breakfast in Cape Elizabeth
Leave the hotel by ~8:15 — an easy 35–40 min down I-295. Show up rested, not road-weary.
10:30a
Portland Head Light · Fort Williams Parkhost pick
Locked in: Maine's most photographed lighthouse, ~10 min from breakfast. Easy clifftop paths through Fort Williams Park, lobster-roll food trucks on site. An Old Port stroll or Crescent Beach walk still fits after if there's energy — then get into lodging / drop bags before the ferry.
2:15p
Casco Bay Lines ferry → Peaks Island
56 Commercial St. Park the Jeep in a garage, walk on the ferry (~17 min crossing, roughly hourly). Aim for a ~2:45–3:15 boat — an hour of island wandering before the party, and missing one ferry still gets you there on time.
4:30–8:30 PM 🦞 · Attire: casual beach cocktail
Welcome Lobster Bake — Greenwood Garden, Peaks Island
2 Garden Pl, Portland, ME 04108
Fresh lobster, corn on the cob, ocean views, summer sunset. Breezy and beachy. Last ferry back is late evening — check the schedule.
