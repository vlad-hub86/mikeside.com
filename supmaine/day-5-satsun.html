In town: Eastern Prom Trail + Back Cove loop
Flat, paved, right on the water — the Eastern Prom Trail along Casco Bay linked to the Back Cove loop, ~6–7 mi round trip with skyline and island views. Cross to South Portland for the Bug Light / Spring Point lighthouses if you want more.
≈10–16 mi · ~1–1.5 hr out-and-back

The marshes: Eastern Trail to Scarborough Marsh
Head south on the Eastern Trail — a flat rail-trail through Maine's biggest salt marsh, herons and egrets, boardwalk stretches. Ride as far as the legs want (10–16 mi round trip) and turn back. It's the same marsh as Monday's ride, so save it for one day or the other.
