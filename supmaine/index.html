The big nature day. Acadia's greatest hits in one loop — a cold-water cove, the surf-pounded Ocean Path, popovers on a lawn with a mountain view — then we trade the park for the highway with a dockside lobster dinner on the way south. Long day, zero filler.

10:00a

Start of the Ocean Path. Park pass required — $35/vehicle, buy online before.
11:15a
Ocean Path → Thunder Hole
Walk it from Sand Beach. Loudest 1–2 hrs before high tide.
12:30p
Jordan Pond — popovers + the view
Popovers from the outdoor stand and the classic Bubbles view from the lawn. The 3.3-mile loop got cut for schedule — no regrets, the view is the point.
1:30p
Cadillac Mountain summitreservation
Timed vehicle entry — $6 on recreation.gov, 30-min entry window, stay as long as you like. Highest point on the North Atlantic seaboard. Save the QR code offline — rangers scan it and cell service is spotty. (Plan B: the 7:30p slot catches sunset, at the cost of a ~11pm Freeport arrival.)
